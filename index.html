Our product

Skiing is a fun activity, but the cold of the weather can really take away from the experience. Sure, bundling up in clothing will protect your body, but what about your face! This is why we created the heated ski mask! Simply charge it in your lodge, home, or anywhere else, and stay warm for up to __ hours. You can even adjust the temperature to get it just right!
